Graph Databases

Graph databases have been around for decades, but they recently exploded in popularity due to websites such as Twitter and Facebook. Facebook uses what is known as a social graph to store related information about people and their associates. Some important characteristic of Graph Databases are:

Graph Database Characteristics

  • A Graph Database contains nodes that store data and edges that store relationships between nodes
  • Both nodes and edges can have properties that are used to describe the data and relationships, respectively
  • Users traverse through the nodes and edges to gain insight about inter-connected data and the relationships between nodes. This is the defining characteristic of Graph Databases

Popular Graph Database Implementations

Currently, there are three popular implementations for Graph Databases:
  • Neo4j
  • AllegroGraph
  • GraphDB
